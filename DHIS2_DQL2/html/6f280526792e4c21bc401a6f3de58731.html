Now that you have completed all of the modules, you are now able to take the Final Assessment for this course. The final assessment consists of 11 questions and the assessment comprises 45% of your total grade. Each question is worth one point for a total of 11 points. You have 1 or 2 attempts on each question. This assessment is not timed, so feel free to take your time with it. If you get stuck, you can simply pause, and come back to the question later.
