Set Up - Outliers graded exercise

Open the DHIS2 Data Quality training instance. Open the WHO Data Quality Tool from the applications menu.

Once the WHO Data Quality Tool has loaded, you can begin setting up the application to complete the exercise. You’ll then follow the instructions below:

  1. Click on the tab for the “Outliers” dashboard. In the menu window, ensure the "Malaria" group is selected for the Data. Set the period to the current year and select August 2021. Set the organisation unit boundary to National and select District for the Disaggregation.
  2. Click on the Options button then click on “Outliers”.
  3. Check the box for “Include Z-score”.
  4. Then click “Modified Z-Score” and then click “Extreme”.
